What We Do?

Team Endurance Racing is the official BAJA Club of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune. We are a group of undergraduate students who design and develop All-Terrain Vehicles and participate in various national and international competitions. We are the only club at VIT, Pune, to have participated in international competitions such as SAE BAJA International and various national competitions like SAE BAJA and ATVC. With a talented and hardworking team, we aim to develop high-end vehicles and achieve success in various competitions.
